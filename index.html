Designer Prompts

Paste these into Claude Code to customize your Shopify theme. Each prompt targets a specific section.

Hero
Update your Shopify theme hero section to be full bleed with the headline text overlapping the bottom of the image. Make the text white, bold, left aligned, with a subtle dark gradient overlay on the bottom half of the image. Keep it responsive.
Product Page
Update your Shopify theme product page to use a two column layout on desktop. Left column: product images with a vertical thumbnail strip. Right column: product title, price, description in accordion sections, and add to cart button. Matches a clean editorial fashion brand aesthetic.
Navigation
Update your Shopify theme header to be transparent on the homepage and solid dark on scroll. Add a smooth transition between the two states. Keep all navigation links white.
Banner
Add a full width announcement banner above your Shopify theme header. Make it transparent with a thin bottom border. Text centered, small caps font, white text. Should be dismissible with an X button.
Product Grid
Update your Shopify theme collection grid to show 3 products per row on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile. Add a smooth zoom effect on product image hover. Remove the add to cart button from the grid — show only image, name, and price.
Footer
Redesign your Shopify theme footer to be minimal and dark. Three columns: brand name and one line description left, navigation links center, social icons right. Small copyright line at the very bottom. Clean, no clutter.
Store Inspiration

References from stores that get it right. Study what they do — then steal the principles.

Represent Clothing
Clean editorial product pages, strong brand voice, minimal navigation.
  • Product images take up the full viewport — no distractions
  • Copy is direct and confident, never over-explains
  • Navigation has four items max — no dropdown overload
Asket
Radical transparency in product copy, simple layout, strong about page.
  • Every product shows cost breakdown and material sourcing
  • Homepage is one scroll — no clutter, no pop-ups
  • About page reads like a manifesto, not a corporate blurb
Entireworld
Conversational tone, simple homepage, product descriptions that feel human.
  • Product descriptions talk like a friend, not a marketer
  • Homepage hero is a single image with five words of copy
  • Colour names are playful and brand-specific, not generic
Aime Leon Dore
Lifestyle photography over product shots, minimal text, brand world over product features.
  • Every image sells a feeling, not just a garment
  • Product pages have almost no descriptive copy — the image does the work
  • No discounts, no urgency timers — the brand speaks for itself
Applied Art Forms
Technical copy done beautifully, dark aesthetic, strong size guide.
  • Fabric specs and construction details written with real precision
  • Dark palette used consistently — every page feels intentional
  • Size guide is a full page with fit notes, not just a chart
What they all have in common
  • Clear brand point of view — you know exactly who this is for
  • Minimal navigation — three to five items, nothing buried
  • Strong hero image that sets the tone before you read a word
  • Product descriptions that sound like a person wrote them
  • Fast-loading simple layout — no widgets, no clutter

Email Marketing
Setup

Connect any email marketing platform to your Shopify store. Popular options include Klaviyo, Omnisend, and Mailchimp — all have free tiers and Shopify integrations. Once connected, activate these three flows first.

01Welcome Series
Sends automatically when someone subscribes. First impression — introduce your brand and set the tone before you ask for anything.
02Abandoned Cart
Sends 1 hour after someone leaves without buying. Highest ROI flow in e-commerce — recovers customers who were already interested.
03Post Purchase
Sends after every order to encourage a review or repeat buy. Turns one-time buyers into loyal customers and builds social proof.
